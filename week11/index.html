ABOUT US

Pietro's Restaurant started in 1960 as a small family operated bar and dining establishment with two employees. Through several expansions it has evolved into a restaurant and banquet facility that can accommodate up to 120 people. Still family owned, Pietro's is known for serving generations of families, with quality meals at reasonable prices.
